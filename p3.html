Illustrated Technical Manual


Below are several of pages of a guide created to give users needed information to get started with their new vacuum. The illustrations were created from scratch in Adobe Illustrator, and the page layout was completed in Adobe InDesign. Details within the pages/illustrations can be seen at the bottom images.





The images below reveal details in the illustrations. Brushes were used to enhance the illustrations' perspective and depth. Also, outlines of the illustrations show the detail used to complete the designs.
