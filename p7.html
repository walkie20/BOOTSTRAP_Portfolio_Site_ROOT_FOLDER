Furniture Catalog - Modular Grid Design Layout


This is the design for a furniture catalog layout. The items are arranged on a modular grid. The logo and layouts are original, as is the text. Images are from Thinkstock.com.

The back and front of a catalog cover were laid out in Adobe InDesign for a demonstration on using the modular grid to assist with content placement.
