Keller Cosmetics Catalog Spreads


This makeup catalog was created to help Keller cosmetics rejuvenate their brand. Strong stock images from thinkstock.com were implemented with edited text from a past catalog to refresh the brand image and reach the target audience.

This is a catalog created during a demo on how to use the grid for designing a catalog. In this version, some typefaces were changed and elements were re-aligned.

The golden grid was used to align images and text in this layout. High quality images were obtained through a subscription with Thinkstock.com.
