MOD Hair Design Studio Logo & Print Ads


MOD Hair Design Studio Print Ads were created for a new hair salon. The logo was hand sketched, traced into Adobe Illustrator, formatted, and completed within one hour. The posters were laid out in Adobe Photoshop using images from Thinkstock.com. The images were cropped and manipulated with layout style effects resulting in what is shown below.
