New Body Nutrition Graphic Package Design


The following logo and package label design were created for New Balance Nutrition. This label was used for the company's new line of fitness supplements. The layout and typeface choices were used to complement the client's color preferences and well written copy to promote the product to fitness enthusiasts.

The following labels were created and used by New Body Nutrition's line of fitness supplements. The typefaces were chosen to be clear and legible and the layout directs the viewer's attention to selling points of the product from the copy provided by the client.








The following logo was developed for New Body Nutrition using Adobe Illustrator. The lines and shape of the logo shows movement and action, while the text is bold to reflect dominance. In a more literal way, the logo shape and layout represents a weight barbell and plates.
