Realty 1 Trifold Brochure & Branding


This branding was created for a real estate company in the Charlotte, NC region. The wordmark logo was designed to reflect an established look and feel based on inspiration from type used in 1960's signage.

A trifold brochure that featured homes for sale was created with dynamic images, shapes, textures, and typography that highlight home features to the viewer.
